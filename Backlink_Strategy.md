# Backlink Strategy for hemkumar.dev

To improve Domain Relevance and Authority, generating high-quality backlinks is crucial for SEO.

## 1. Directory & Profile Submissions
- Ensure your GitHub, LinkedIn, and any other professional profiles have links pointing to `https://hemkumar.dev/`.
- Submit your portfolio to CSS/Design galleries like Awwwards, CSS Design Awards, or Web Design Inspiration (your NeoBrutalist design makes you a good fit here).
- List your portfolio on localized directories (e.g., Chennai web developer directories, tech community lists).

## 2. Guest Blogging & Content Contribution
- Write high-quality technical articles on platforms like Dev.to, Hashnode, Medium, and FreeCodeCamp. Include a bio with a link back to your portfolio.
- Reach out to specialized tech blogs in the "NeoBrutalism" or "Frontend Performance" niches and offer a guest post.

## 3. Open Source Contributions
- Contribute to open-source communities. Many projects list contributors with links to their personal websites.
- Sponsor or develop small open-source tools/plugins and host the documentation on a subdomain of `hemkumar.dev`, creating organic references.

## 4. Academic & Event Backlinks
- Since you are part of GDG, DEF CON Chennai, and a Microsoft Student Ambassador, ensure those organization pages (if they have member directories) link to your site.
- If Panimalar Engineering College features alumni or student achievements, request a feature with a backlink.

## 5. Forum & Community Engagement
- Participate actively in Reddit (e.g., r/webdev, r/Frontend), Stack Overflow, and Indie Hackers. Do not spam, but include your portfolio naturally when showing examples of your work or in your profile signatures.
